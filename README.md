# Customer-Subscription-Churn-Analysis
This project analyzes customer churn behavior for a subscription-based digital service using Power BI. The objective is to identify churn patterns, key risk factors, and revenue insights that can help businesses take early retention actions.

**Dataset Summary**
Total Customers: 2,800
Granularity: One row per customer

**Key Features:**

Subscription plan & monthly fee
Usage intensity and engagement
Payment failures
Customer tenure
Churn indicator

Key KPIs

Total Customers
Churn Rate (%)
Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
Churned Customers Count
Dashboard Components

**Churn Overview**

Overall churn rate and customer count
Churn vs retained customer distribution
Churn trend by tenure group
Churn by subscription plan
Revenue & Pricing Analysis
Monthly fee distribution
Revenue contribution by plan
Churn rate by monthly fee bands
Risk & Behavior Analysis
Churn rate by payment failures
Usage intensity vs churn (scatter analysis)
Tenure-based churn patterns
Decomposition Tree to identify churn drivers

**Key Insights**

Customers with short tenure contribute the highest share of churn.
Payment failures are one of the strongest churn predictors.
Low usage intensity is an early behavioral signal of churn.
Churn rates are relatively similar across plans, indicating pricing alone does not prevent churn.
Revenue concentration is higher in Premium plans, increasing the impact of churn in those segments.
